Roujiamou Recipe

Juicy authentic roujiamou(Chinese hamburger)

This recipe is as authentic as it gets. Succulent pork is magically spiced while the bread is baked. Mix them together and you have the best burger you've never tried.

Ingredients

  • 5lb fatty pork shoulder
  • Five spice powder
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • 10 finely chopped onions
  • burger buns

Steps

  1. Chop pork shoulder into quarters
  2. Marinate pork in other ingredients for 3hours
  3. Bake burger buns slowly at 120C until nice and dry
  4. Slowly roast meat at 120C for 3hours
  5. Chop meat finely then fill your buns. Enjoy!
