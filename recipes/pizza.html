Pizza Recipe

Classic super cheesy pizza. Simple fluffy and crispy dough slathered with tomato sauce and swamped with gooey Mozzarella.

This recipe is all about simplicity without compromising flavour. Nothing too fancy, just proper execution which leads to an explosion of flavour.

Ingredients

For the Dough
  • 250ml water
  • 300g flour
  • Pinch salt
  • Just a lil bit of olive oil(A gallon).
For the Toppings
  • 1kg creamy mozzarella di Italia
  • 2tbsp tomato puree
  • Drizzle of olive oil

Steps

  1. Knead the dough ingredients for 30m
  2. Let the dough rest under a cloth for 3hours until doubled in size
  3. Form into a pizza shape then add tomato sauce
  4. Top with all the cheese and more if necessary
  5. Finish with olive oil. Bake for 2m at 1000C
